Deodorant sprays and shave creams: missing hazard labels

Last updated: June 27, 2018 view history

Summary

  • Products: Deodorant sprays and shave creams – Axe, Brut, Dove, Gillette, Nivea
  • Issue: Missing the required hazard labelling
  • What to do: Stop using them and contact the distributor below to arrange a return

Affected products

Check the name of your product and its UPC to see if it’s affected by this recall. NOTE: If your product has the hazard image above on the label, then it is not part of this recall, even if it’s listed below.

Issue

The recalled products do not have mandatory hazard labelling. Improper labelling could result in misuse of the product and lead to serious injury.

The company has not received any reports of incidents or injuries related to these products in Canada as of November 7, 2018.

What you should do

Stop using the recalled product(s) and contact MPS Canada Corporation to arrange a return at 905-886-4437 ext 112 between 9:30 am and 5:30 pm (Mon-Fri) and by email.
