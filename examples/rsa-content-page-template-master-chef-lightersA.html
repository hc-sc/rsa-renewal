MASTER Chef BBQ lighters: fire and burn hazard

MASTER Chef

Last updated: June 27, 2018 view history

Summary

  • Product: MASTER Chef BBQ Igniter lighters
  • Issue: May pose a fire and/or burn hazard
  • What to do: Stop using them and return to a Canadian Tire store for a refund

Affected products

The following model is affected by this recall:
Product number Lot number Manufacturing date UPC
076-2044 AD6523/G35J 2016-02-01 645990215739

The product, lot and UPC numbers are on the back of the packaging. If you don't have the original packaging, use the manufacturing date located on the label on the lighter handle.

Issue

These lighters may pose a fire or burn hazard by:

  • exceeding the allowable flame height
  • producing an abnormal, sudden increase in flame height
  • continuing to burn after the trigger has been released

The company has received no reports of incidents or injuries in Canada as of June 22, 2018.

What you should do

Immediately stop using the recalled lighters and return them to a Canadian Tire store for a refund.

For more information, contact Canadian Tire Corporation by telephone at 1-800-387-8803 or by email.

Do not redistribute, sell or even give away recalled products. It is illegal under the Canada Consumer Product Safety Act.
