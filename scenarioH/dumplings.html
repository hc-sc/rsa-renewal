Liao Bu De Fish and Shrimp Dumplings: undeclared egg

Recall date: April 10, 2018

Liao Bu De

Summary

  • Product: Liao Bu De Fish and Shrimp Dumplings
  • Issue: Undeclared egg
  • What to do: Throw out the product if you are allergic to eggs

Affected products

Check the UPC code on your product.

Brand name Common name Size UPC Code on product
Liao Bu De Fish and Shrimp Dumplings 230 g 6 953640 000623 All lot codes where egg is not declared on the label

Issue

Legend Q Trading Inc. is recalling Liao Bu De brand Fish and Shrimp Dumplings from the marketplace because it may contain egg which is not declared on the label.

What you should do

If you are allergic to eggs, you should throw out or return the product.

Similar recalls

  • Golden Ocean Inc. brand and Liao Bu De brand fish products recalled due to undeclared egg

Background

This recall was triggered by the company. The Canadian Food Inspection Agency (CFIA) is conducting a food safety investigation, which may lead to the recall of other products. If other high-risk products are recalled, the CFIA will notify the public through updated Food Recall Warnings.

The CFIA is verifying that industry is removing recalled product from the marketplace.
